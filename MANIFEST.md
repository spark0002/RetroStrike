## Why This Matters (2024-2026 Context)

The global tech industry is experiencing unprecedented mass layoffs while reporting record profits. Corporations are using "AI transformation" as justification to eliminate workers — the same workers whose labor, data, and creativity trained the AI systems now replacing them. This is not a natural market correction. This is engineered inequality.

Key facts:
- Major tech companies have laid off hundreds of thousands of workers since 2023 while posting record revenue
- CEO compensation has grown 1,000%+ over 40 years while worker wages stagnated
- AI training data was largely created by the public — yet the public is being excluded from AI's benefits
- The cost of replicating software with AI has dropped dramatically — corporate moats built on talent density are dissolving

## Core Principles

1. **We are not anti-AI** — We are anti-abuse-of-AI
2. **Monitor → Warn → RetroStrike** — A graduated response framework
3. **Build, don't destroy** — We create open-source alternatives through independent development
4. **Respect IP** — All replacement projects are built from scratch, not copied
5. **Transparency** — All code is open, all actions are public
